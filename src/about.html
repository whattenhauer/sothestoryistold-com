About Us

So The Story Is Told was created with one simple goal:

To help people turn their heartfelt words and personal stories into beautiful, illustrated animated reels — like digital greeting cards that come to life.

How it works

  1. You write your short story or message
  2. Choose the style and occasion
  3. Our AI turns it into a storyboard with illustrations
  4. You get a ready-to-share animated reel (YouTube Shorts style)

Why we built this

Because some messages deserve more than plain text.
Whether it's a birthday wish, words of encouragement, a love note, or a proud parent moment — your story deserves to be told in a memorable way.
